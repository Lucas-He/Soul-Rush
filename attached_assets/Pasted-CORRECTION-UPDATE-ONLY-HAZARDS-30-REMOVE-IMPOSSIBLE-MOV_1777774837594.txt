REMOVE IMPOSSIBLE OR WAY TOO HARD MOVES

Some boss moves are impossible or way too hard. Remove or debuff all unfair cases.

Global Debuff Rules:
- No unavoidable attacks.
- No full-arena attacks without a clear safe zone.
- No overlapping hazards that cover every escape route.
- No control reversal combined with dense bullet spam.
- No shrinking arena combined with rotating lasers and heavy bullet rain unless there is a large readable safe pocket.
- No visibility reduction combined with fake warnings and fast bullets at the same time.
- No delayed movement combined with precise narrow gaps.
- No random hazard spawning directly on the player.
- No hazards spawning without warning.
- No safe gap smaller than the player hitbox plus a reasonable margin.
- No more than 3 heavy mechanics layered at once except Boss 20 final phase, and even there each phase must be short and readable.

Safe Gap Rules:
- Minimum safe gap should be at least 2.5x the player hitbox diameter for Bosses 1–5.
- Minimum safe gap should be at least 2.0x the player hitbox diameter for Bosses 6–14.
- Minimum safe gap should be at least 1.6x the player hitbox diameter for Bosses 15–19.
- Minimum safe gap should be at least 1.5x the player hitbox diameter for Boss 20.
- If hazards move quickly, increase the safe gap.
- If player movement is altered, increase the safe gap.

Warning Time Rules:
- Bosses 1–5: warning time should usually be 0.9–1.2 seconds.
- Bosses 6–10: warning time should usually be 0.75–1.0 seconds.
- Bosses 11–15: warning time should usually be 0.6–0.9 seconds.
- Bosses 16–19: warning time should usually be 0.55–0.8 seconds.
- Boss 20: warning time should usually be 0.5–0.75 seconds.
- Never go below 0.45 seconds for major hazards.

Layering Rules:
- Bosses 1–5: maximum 2 layers per wave.
- Bosses 6–10: maximum 2–3 layers per wave.
- Bosses 11–15: maximum 3 layers per wave.
- Bosses 16–19: maximum 3 layers, rarely 4 for short bursts.
- Boss 20: maximum 4 layers, and only for short readable phases.
- Do not stack:
  - control changes + visibility loss + fake warnings
  - gravity pull + shrinking arena + dense lasers
  - poison zones + moving safe pocket + fast bullets + screen glitch
  - delayed movement + small gaps
  - fake warnings + darkness + fast hazards
